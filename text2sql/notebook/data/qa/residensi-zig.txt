Residensi ZIG

Residensi ZIG is the new phase in Kiara Bay, Kepong, Kuala Lumpur . Different and bold, this trendy high-rise development showcases flexible living features with exciting indoor and awesome outdoor living spaces. It is a design inspired by nature, aspires to connect to its surrounding blues and greens. A perfect tale about urbanity meets nature.

Brimming with features and highly attainable, Residensi ZIG offers urban liveability a mere 7-minute walk to The Beat and the Kepong Metropolitan Park lakeside.
