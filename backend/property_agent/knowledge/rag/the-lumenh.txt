The LUMENH

Introducing The Lumenh at Mutiara Damansara, the epitome of modern luxury living. This exclusive development offers a unique blend of high-end features
and hospitality-inspired environments. Each residence in The Club Edition is thoughtfully
designed to cater to the discerning tastes of the modern resident.

A true masterpiece of green architecture, The Lumenh has been awarded Provisional Gold GreenRE
Certification for its commitment to sustainability. Located adjacent to Mutiara Damansara, this
elevated site boasts breathtaking views and a unique topography that naturally preserves the
natural surroundings.

Our luxurious detached villas and twin villas are designed to maximize natural light and
ventilation, creating a sense of serenity and tranquility within each residence. With only 80
units per acre, residents can enjoy absolute privacy and comfort in their spacious homes.

From unit types A and B with bungalow-style designs to C and D with Semi-D-style layouts, every
detail has been carefully crafted to provide an unparalleled luxury living experience. The
Lumenh at Mutiara Damansara is a low-density residence that combines stunning aesthetics with
functional design, inviting residents to indulge in the ultimate resort-living experience.

Here, every moment feels like a vacation.
