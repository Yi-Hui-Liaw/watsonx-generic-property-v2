ABC-Residence

ABC-Residence is the new phase in Damansara Bay, Mutiara Damansara, Kuala Lumpur . Different and bold, this trendy high-rise development showcases flexible living features with exciting indoor and awesome outdoor living spaces. It is a design inspired by nature, aspires to connect to its surrounding blues and greens. A perfect tale about urbanity meets nature.

Brimming with features and highly attainable, ABC-Residence offers urban liveability a mere 7-minute walk to The Beat and the Mutiara Damansara Metro Park lakeside.
