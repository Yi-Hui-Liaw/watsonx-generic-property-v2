The Connaught One

Introducing The Connaught One, the first development to be launched under The RISE Series by UEM Sunrise that embodies practical living with an environment that exudes simplicity, recreation and convenience.Located at Jalan 3/144a, Cheras, 56000 Kuala Lumpur.

The Connaught One is an exciting new freehold Transit-Oriented Development designed for maximum convenience. Strategically located next to Taman Connaught MRT Station, everything is within easy reach. Units are flexibly sized between 42 sqm to 118 sqm (452 sqft to 1,270 sqft) with up to 4 bedrooms, perfect for young couples and growing families. With a price starting from only RM290K*, register now to secure your unit.Targeted for first-time homebuyers, professionals as well as multi-generational families, The Connaught One will offer two 53-storey towers, Tower A and B that will comprise 1,334 service apartment units and 15 retail lots.
